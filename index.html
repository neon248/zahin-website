Hello. I am Farhan Ishraque Zahin from Bangladesh. I graduated from Islamic University of Technology (IUT) with honors in Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE) in March, 2021. I am enthusiastic about learning new things and applying science to enrich the world. My undergraduate thesis was on optimization of light trapping in Gallium Arsenide thin film solar cells using Aluminum nanoparticles under the supervision of Professor Dr. Md. Ruhul Amin, Head of EEE department of IUT. I am passionate about teaching science and have taught physics, chemistry and math to students ranging from grade 8 to 12. I am also a recreational powerlifter, and I occasionally compete in powerlifting contests. It helps me push my body and mind to it's limits and grow physically as well as mentally. In the coming years of my life, I see myself contributing to high quality research and helping in technological advancements that will solve some of the challenging problems of the world. I want to devote my career to the pursuit of knowledge and and using it to help humanity.
